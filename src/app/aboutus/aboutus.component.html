About Us

Journal of Cardiac Interventions (JCI) is an international journal which spans the field of cardiovascular diseases. JCI covers the length and breadth of cardiac and peripheral interventions and therapeutics and publishes all type of articles in the concerned field.

The editors encourage authors to submit excellent articles on diagnosis, management, and treatment of Cardiovascular Diseases and other related fields. The journal also deals with basic science articles such as those related to anatomy, physiology, pathology, and genetics of Cardiovascular Diseases. Categories of articles accepted include Original Articles, Reviews, Case Reports, Images in Cardiology, case challenges, Editorials and Letters to the Editors. Please read the following instructions before you prepare the manuscript. Submitted manuscripts are peer reviewed and evaluated by two (or more) experts of the field. Acceptance decisions are made based solely on the quality of data, originality of ideas, and presentation.

Publication Frequency

Journal of Cardiac Interventions is published two times per year.

New Policy: Expedited Publication Decision

Additionally, JCI provides an Expedited Publication track for original work that communicates novel, influential and clinically impactful research.

Peer Review Process

Manuscripts will be submitted to two reviewers for evaluation of their significance and soundness. Authors will be notified at every significant step in the peer-reviewing process.

Open Access Policy

This journal provides open access to all of it content on the principle that making research freely available to the public supports a greater global exchange of knowledge. Such access is associated with increased readership and increased citation of an author's work.

Why publish here?

Journal of Cardiac Interventions publishes papers covering the broad field of cardiovascular diseases.

The priority of the journal is to promote research and increase knowledge on Cardiac Interventions, both in the theoretical and applied field.

In its new electronic format, Journal of Cardiac Interventions guarantees:

Online-First: rapid online visibility after acceptance

Open Access: papers are available to the entire scientific community at no charge

No page limit: large amount of space to publish extensive papers

Supplemental material: sounds, short videos or original datasets can be published online

Please contact editor@journal-of-cardiac-interventions.com for further information.
